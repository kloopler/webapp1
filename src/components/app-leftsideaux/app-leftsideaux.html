1 - Add Photos

Click the "Step 1 - Add Photos" button in the left-handside toolbar

Click on one of the photos revealed in the middle pane to place on the board

Click and drag the photo on the board to an appropriate location within the board


2 - Save Board

Click the "Step 2 - Save" button in the left-handside toolbar.

A dialog will pop up allowing you to enter or edit the title and description of your board. Click "Save" to save your edits.


3 - Preview Board

Click the "Step 3 - Preview" button in the left-handside toolbar.

Playback of the current items on the board will begin
